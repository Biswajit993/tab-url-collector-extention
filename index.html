Privacy Policy for Tab URL Collector

Effective Date: December 13, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Tab URL Collector! This extension is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we ensure its security and privacy.

Information We Collect

The extension collects the following information:

  • Tab URLs: The URLs of all open tabs in your Chrome browser window.

No other personal, sensitive, or identifiable information is collected by the extension.

How We Use Your Data

The collected URLs are used solely for the following purposes:

  • To display the list of open tab URLs within the extension interface.
  • To allow users to copy the collected URLs for personal use.

The extension operates locally in your browser and does not transmit or store any data on external servers.

Third-Party Access

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties under any circumstances.

User Control

You have full control over the use of this extension. The URLs are displayed only for your convenience and are not saved or transmitted anywhere. If you uninstall the extension, all associated data will no longer be accessible.
